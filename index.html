ALL ABOUT ME

MOLLIE RESNICK

The first vacation I ever went on was with my family out west. We went to California, Utah, Nevada and many more. One place that we went was the Sequoais National Park in California. These trees amazed me because of their great size and beauty.




Another place I went on this vacation was the Grand Canyon. The Grand Canyon was so large that I was in shock and it made me think about how small I was. All I could think about was the thought what if I could fly? Then I could jump in and fly wherever I wanted to. That has always been my biggest fantasy.




The first country I visited outside of the United States and Canada was Costa Rica. I went to Costa Rica with my family when I was ten years old. When my family goes on vacation, we do not go to touristy places, instead we stay where the locals live, somewhat. I love traveling, because I love going to the beach, eating exotic foods and speaking with the local people of each destination. Family vacations are my favorite things.
